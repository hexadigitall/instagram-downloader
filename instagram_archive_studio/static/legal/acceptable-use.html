Legal

Acceptable Use

Use Instagram Archive Studio only for legitimate archiving workflows where you have the right to save the content.

Allowed Uses

Examples include archiving your own posts, saving assets for a brand account you manage, preserving client-approved UGC, maintaining an offline content library, and exporting job records for audit or operations.

Disallowed Uses

Do not use this tool to harvest accounts, bypass access controls, collect private content without permission, infringe copyrights, impersonate users, resell downloaded media without rights, or run large-scale automated collection without authorization.

Platform Rules

Meta's terms restrict unauthorized automated access and collection. Keep workflows narrow, permission-based, and compliant with the rules that apply to you.

Abuse Response

Before public launch, add an abuse-report process, takedown process, account suspension policy, and escalation workflow for rights holders and platform complaints.
